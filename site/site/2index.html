Minecraft.

Minecraft is a boundless sandbox where your imagination shapes the world. Picture a pixelated universe made of blocks—every mountain, cave, and castle crafted by your hands. Whether you're battling creepers in survival mode, crafting diamond armor, or building sky-high cities in creative mode, the game is pure freedom. With redstone engineering, magic portals, hidden biomes, and infinite worlds, it's a realm where kids become architects, adventurers, and inventors. Play solo or with friends, ride dolphins, explore the Nether, or slay the Ender Dragon. In Minecraft, there are no limits—just endless adventures waiting in every blocky corner.

The big challenge in the End is fighting the Ender Dragon. It’s the final boss of Minecraft, and to defeat it, you’ll need to destroy several Ender Crystals on top of obsidian pillars, which heal the dragon. Once those are taken out, you can start attacking the dragon itself. It can be tricky, as it flies around and does a lot of damage!
